## 🧩 Topic 11: Abstract Concepts (rất hay gặp trong Writing Task 2)

| Word | Type | Meaning (VN) | Collocations | Example Sentence |
|------|------|--------------|--------------|------------------|
| abstract | adj | trừu tượng | abstract idea, abstract concept | Freedom is an abstract concept. |
| concept | noun | khái niệm | key concept, basic concept | This is a key concept in psychology. |
| notion | noun | quan niệm | common notion, traditional notion | The notion of success varies. |
| perspective | noun | góc nhìn | different perspectives, from a perspective | We should consider different perspectives. |
| framework | noun | khuôn khổ | theoretical framework, legal framework | The theory provides a framework for analysis. |
| dimension | noun | khía cạnh | social dimension, economic dimension | There are many dimensions to this issue. |
| aspect | noun | khía cạnh | important aspect, key aspect | Cost is an important aspect. |
| paradigm | noun | mô hình tư duy | paradigm shift, traditional paradigm | Technology caused a paradigm shift. |
| entity | noun | thực thể | separate entity, legal entity | The company is a legal entity. |
| perception | noun | nhận thức | public perception, change perception | Media shapes public perception. |

## ✍️ Topic 12: Argument & Essay Writing (core IELTS)

| Word | Type | Meaning (VN) | Collocations | Example Sentence |
|------|------|--------------|--------------|------------------|
| argue | verb | tranh luận | argue that, strongly argue | Many argue that education is key. |
| claim | noun/verb | tuyên bố | make a claim, claim that | He claims that this is effective. |
| assert | verb | khẳng định | assert confidently, assert that | The author asserts that this is true. |
| maintain | verb | giữ quan điểm | maintain that, maintain position | She maintains that change is needed. |
| suggest | verb | đề xuất | suggest that, strongly suggest | Studies suggest that sleep is vital. |
| demonstrate | verb | chứng minh | clearly demonstrate, demonstrate evidence | The results demonstrate a clear trend. |
| support | verb | ủng hộ | strongly support, support argument | Data supports this theory. |
| oppose | verb | phản đối | strongly oppose, oppose idea | Many oppose this policy. |
| refute | verb | bác bỏ | refute argument, refute claim | He refuted the argument effectively. |
| justify | verb | biện minh | justify decision, justify actions | The government justified its policy. |
