## 🧾 Topic 16: Formal Verbs (thay thế từ basic)

| Basic Word | Academic Word | Meaning (VN) | Collocations | Example Sentence |
|------------|--------------|--------------|--------------|------------------|
| get | obtain | đạt được | obtain results, obtain data | Researchers obtained accurate results. |
| show | demonstrate | chứng minh | clearly demonstrate, demonstrate effect | The study demonstrates a clear trend. |
| find | identify | xác định | identify problem, identify pattern | Scientists identified key issues. |
| use | utilize | sử dụng | utilize resources, utilize method | We should utilize available resources. |
| help | assist | hỗ trợ | assist development, assist students | Technology assists learning. |
| make | generate | tạo ra | generate income, generate results | The system generates data. |
| fix | resolve | giải quyết | resolve issue, resolve conflict | The government resolved the problem. |
| improve | enhance | cải thiện | enhance performance, enhance quality | This method enhances efficiency. |
| speed up | accelerate | tăng tốc | accelerate growth, accelerate process | Technology accelerates progress. |
| reduce | minimize | giảm thiểu | minimize risk, minimize cost | We must minimize errors. |

|------|------|--------------|--------------|------------------|
| state | verb | tuyên bố | clearly state, state that | The report states that pollution is rising. |
| indicate | verb | chỉ ra | indicate that, clearly indicate | Results indicate improvement. |
| reveal | verb | tiết lộ | reveal that, reveal findings | The study reveals new insights. |
| suggest | verb | gợi ý | suggest that, strongly suggest | Data suggests a correlation. |
| argue | verb | lập luận | argue that, strongly argue | Experts argue that change is needed. |
| claim | verb | khẳng định | claim that, widely claim | Some claim that this is effective. |

## 🏗️ Topic 18: Passive Voice & Academic Tone

| Structure | Meaning (VN) | Example Sentence |
|----------|--------------|------------------|
| It is believed that... | Người ta tin rằng... | It is believed that education is essential. |
| It is argued that... | Người ta cho rằng... | It is argued that technology improves life. |
| It is widely accepted that... | Được công nhận rộng rãi | It is widely accepted that exercise is beneficial. |
| It can be seen that... | Có thể thấy rằng | It can be seen that trends are changing. |
| It should be noted that... | Cần lưu ý rằng | It should be noted that data is limited. |
| It is evident that... | Rõ ràng là | It is evident that pollution is increasing. |
| It is often claimed that... | Thường được cho là | It is often claimed that money brings happiness. |
| It has been suggested that... | Đã được đề xuất rằng | It has been suggested that sleep is important. |

| bring about changes | mang lại thay đổi | Policies bring about positive changes. |
| give rise to | dẫn đến | This issue gives rise to conflict. |

| be attributed to | được quy cho | Success is attributed to hard work. |

| in terms of | về mặt | In terms of cost, this is efficient. |
| from a broader perspective | từ góc nhìn rộng hơn | From a broader perspective, this is beneficial. |
